Our Story

Introducing ChowChow, a new leaf, showcases inventive cuisine by complementing the flavours and traditions local and international ingredients and techniques. Full of flavor and creativity with every dish, ChowChow offers an exquisite and progressive Indian dining experience along with artisanal cocktails and boutique wines.

Complementing the elevation of nature design inspired by the city of Lagos, the dynamic, young team of chefs under the able guidance of Chef Harsh Dayalani, have skillfully crafted a new culinary experience that engages the roots of Indian cuisine with a modern twist with use of local fresh Nigerian produce.
